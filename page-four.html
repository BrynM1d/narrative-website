The Great Orme

Alongside the bronze age mines the Great Orme has a lot to do and is a must see for any tourist. At the peak of the Orme there is a café called the summit with a giftshop inside. There is also the cable car that will take you to the bottom of the Great Orme by the beach or vice versa! Keep in mind if you would like to ride the cable car they are whether dependent so if it is too windy they won’t run. However, if you would like to visit when it’s too windy you can always get the tram which operate 7 days a week. They have an 18-hole mini golf right outside of the front of the cafe which is fun family activity in the summer. But, if none of these appeals to you the views definitely will! From the top of the Great Orme you get an amazing view of Llandudno and the surrounding areas.
